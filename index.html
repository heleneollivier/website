            <h2>About Me</h2>

            <p>I am a Professor at Paris School of Economics (PSE) and a Research Fellow at the CNRS  (Centre National de la Recherche Scientifique). I am also a Co-Editor at the Journal of Environmental Economics and Management (JEEM), starting from January 2025.

From 2020 to 2024, I served as an Associate Editor at the Environment and Development Economics (EDE) journal. Previously, I was also part of the editorial committee of the Working Papers of the FAERE and part of the editorial council of JEEM.

As an Environmental Economist, my main research interests are related to the evaluation of the effects of environmental policies on manufacturing firms or the effects of environmental shocks (such as air pollution or temperature), in a world where units (firms, countries, agents) interact with each other. 

Other website: Researchgate

I completed my PhD in 2010 at the Economics department at the Ecole Polytechnique, under the direction of Jean-Marc Bourgeon. After that, I was a Ciriacy-Wantrup fellow at the Agricultural and Resource Economics department, at UC Berkeley in 2010-2012. I defended my HDR in 2018.</p>
